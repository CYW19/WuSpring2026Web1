Three Animation Experiments

FLICKER

This animation primarily features a flickering effect. By adjusting the colors and timing, I made the circles appear and disappear at varying intervals, creating a neon display in the colors of the rainbow.

FADE

This animation uses opacity to create a fade-in and fade-out effect. The shapes gradually appear and disappear at different points in time, creating a smooth and rhythmic visual experience. I drew inspiration from flashing traffic lights.

MAGIC

This animation consists of rectangles and squares of varying colors, with different delay times and movement directions. The rectangles move up and down at different speeds, creating an irregular visual effect. I drew inspiration from Tetris.
